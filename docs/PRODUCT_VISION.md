# Product Vision: ClassManager
## The Strategic Roadmap for Classroom Stewardship

This document details the market positioning, strategic vision, and competitive differentiation of ClassManager. It serves as a guide for product managers, designers, and engineers to understand *why* the product exists and *where* it is going over the next five years.

---

## 1. Vision & Mission Statements

### Vision Statement
To transform school management from a clerical database chore into an active, objective, and developmental stewardship experience that fosters healthy, self-governing student communities.

### Mission Statement
To empower homeroom teachers with an observation-first workspace that simplifies daily logging, structures student accountability, and generates objective, defensible evaluations.

---

## 2. Product Positioning

ClassManager sits at the intersection of **Daily Classroom Activity** and **Formal Academic Evaluation**. It is not a broad administrative suite or a passive gradebook; it is a specialized workspace for the **Homeroom Teacher (Giáo viên chủ nhiệm)**.

```
                  [ HIGH-LEVEL ERP / SIS SYSTEMS ]
                        (Passive, Compliance)
                                 ▲
                                 │  Aggregated Reports
                                 │
                     ==> [ CLASSMANAGER ] <==
                   (Active, Observation-First)
                                 ▲
                                 │  Daily Observations
                                 │
                     [ DAILY CLASSROOM INTERACTIONS ]
                        (Attendance, Behavior)
```

## 4. Target Users & Core Problems

### Primary User: Homeroom Teacher (Giáo viên chủ nhiệm)
*   **The Problem:** Homeroom teachers spend hours every weekend manually collecting paper checklists from group leaders, calculating weekly point subtractions, resolving disputes, and compiling reports.
*   **The Pain:** At the end of the semester, they must assign a conduct rating (*Hạnh kiểm*) based on memory or sketchy paper notes, leading to parent disputes and subjective bias.

### Secondary User: Student Group Leaders (Tổ trưởng)
*   **The Problem:** Student leaders must record daily performance data for their peers in physical notebooks. These notebooks are easily lost, damaged, or altered, causing class friction.
*   **The Pain:** Entering student logs is slow and prone to errors.

---

## 5. Value Proposition
ClassManager automates the administrative overhead of classroom stewardship. It turns chaotic paper notebooks and subjective impressions into a clean, objective **Observation Ledger**. Teachers save hours of weekly reporting time, student leaders input logs in seconds, and parents receive clear, evidence-backed reports of their children's behavioral and social development.

---

## 6. Strategic Pillars

1.  **Observation-First Architecture:** The core loop of the app centers on capturing a behavior and linking it to a student. All other modules are rollups of this raw data.
2.  **The Weekly Temporal Rhythm:** The platform drives the teacher toward the Weekly Closeout Ceremony. Time is organized in clean weekly intervals to give students regular opportunities to start fresh.
3.  **Objective Evaluation (Defensibility):** Every final rating is backed by chronological logs. If a parent disputes a grade, the teacher can retrieve a clean record of dates, times, and context logs.

---

## 7. Roadmap (3-5 Year Vision)

### 3-Year Vision: The Standard for Vietnamese Homerooms
*   Expand to support deep parent-teacher communication loops (exporting weekly reports, digital signatures).
*   Incorporate offline-first logging capabilities for classrooms with limited internet connectivity.
*   Standardize the *Tổ-Column* team management and peer-auditing workflows across major schools.

### 5-Year Vision: The Predictive Growth Engine
*   Integrate early-intervention analysis (identifying students showing early signs of social withdrawal or academic decline based on long-term observation patterns).
*   Offer counselor-specific portals to allow school psychologists and counselors to coordinate behavioral support plans with homeroom teachers directly inside the dossier ledger.
*   Provide a standardized data integration API to feed conduct outcomes directly into national school database systems, eliminating double data entry.
